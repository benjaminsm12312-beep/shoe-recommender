Best Cushioned Running Shoes

Cushion is what stands between your legs and the pavement, and more of it means a softer landing and fresher legs over long or easy miles. But not all cushion feels the same — some shoes are pillowy and plush, others are tall but bouncy and springy. The right one depends on whether you want to sink in or push off. If you're running longer distances, recovering between hard days, or you just prefer a soft ride underfoot, a high or max-cushion shoe is the way to go. Below are the softest, most protective shoes we recommend, spanning plush all the way to springy, so you can match the feel to what your legs actually want.

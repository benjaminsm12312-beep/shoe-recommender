Best Walking Shoes

Walking shoes don't get the attention running shoes do, but the right pair makes a real difference — whether you're exploring a new city, walking the dogs every morning, or just on your feet a lot. For walking you want comfort that lasts, a stable and supportive base, and cushion that stays comfortable over the long haul rather than feeling mushy. Many great running shoes double as excellent walkers, which is why you'll see some familiar names below alongside dedicated walking picks. All of them are built to keep your feet happy well past the point where a cheap shoe would have you aching.

These picks come from hands-on shoe-fitting experience, not brand deals. Some links below are affiliate links — if you buy through one we may earn a small commission at no cost to you, and it never changes what we recommend.
